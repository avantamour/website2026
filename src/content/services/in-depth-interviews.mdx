An in-depth interview is an open-ended, one-to-one conversation that uncovers a person's detailed thoughts, motivations, frustrations, and the hidden reasons behind their behaviour. It's the right tool when you need deep qualitative insight — especially around user behaviour, emotion, decision-making, and low adoption.

We conducted 120 in-depth interviews for an OTT platform to understand how people choose content at the moment of discovery.
