A WhatsApp diary study asks participants to record their experiences, actions, and frustrations over time — on the platform they already live in. They share real-life moments, photos, videos, and voice notes with us over days or weeks. Use it when you want to understand real behaviour as it unfolds, and surface patterns a single interview can't catch.

We ran a WhatsApp diary study with 50 customers over five to ten days, capturing what frustrated them as they used a particular app.
