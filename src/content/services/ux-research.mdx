UX research studies users to understand their needs, behaviours, and expectations — who they are, what they're trying to do, where they struggle, why they avoid a feature, and what would make a product easier and more useful. It's recommended mainly for digital products.

We've run UX research studies for clients across IT and transport, among other sectors.
